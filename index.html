About us

Compassionate support, naturally focused.

We guide families toward meaningful, ecological farewells that minimize environmental impact while honoring culture, faith, and personal wishes. Our team blends professional funeral care with a deep respect for the land, using natural materials, low-impact processes, and gentle, honest communication.

Why ecological
Lighter on the earth

Traditional funerals often involve chemicals, concrete, and high energy use. We focus on natural burial, reduced emissions, and biodegradable materials so your choices support the health of future generations.

For families
Calm, clear guidance

Whether you are planning ahead or facing an unexpected loss, we take time to explain options in plain language, support your decisions, and quietly handle the practical details in the background.

For communities
Rooted in place

We collaborate with local woodlands, natural burial grounds, and artisans to keep care close to home, support small producers, and create ceremonies that reflect the landscapes you love.

Services

Ecological funeral and memorial options.

Every family and every life is unique. We offer flexible, modular services that can be combined to create a farewell that feels honest, gentle, and aligned with your environmental values.

Natural burial
Woodland & meadow burials

Simple, unembalmed burials using biodegradable coffins or shrouds, in protected natural spaces that encourage biodiversity instead of concrete.

  • Natural or woodland burial grounds
  • Locally made wicker, wood, or cardboard coffins
  • Simple linen or organic cotton shrouds
  • Low-impact grave marking (stones, trees, or GPS)
Ceremony
Gentle, personal ceremonies

From quiet family gatherings to larger community memorials, we support ceremonies that feel authentic, with space for silence, music, readings, and ritual drawn from your own traditions.

  • Outdoor and indoor ceremony coordination
  • Support for faith-based and non-religious services
  • Integration of eco-rituals (tree planting, river blessings)
  • Recommended celebrants, musicians, and readers
Cremation & ashes
Lower-impact cremation choices

When cremation is chosen, we help you reduce its footprint and find gentle ways to return ashes to the earth through natural memorials and biodegradable vessels.

  • Guidance on lower-emission cremation options where available
  • Biodegradable urns and scattering tubes
  • Forest, garden, and water-based ash scattering
  • Living memorials such as tree or habitat projects
Our approach

Quietly sustainable, deeply human.

Choosing an ecological funeral does not mean compromising on care or meaningful ritual. It means gently aligning each step with your values: from how we care for the body to the materials we use and the places we gather.

We prioritize transparency, low-impact choices, and collaboration with families. You decide what feels right; we bring practical knowledge, emotional steadiness, and trusted partners to make it possible.

Our ecological commitments include:

  • No routine embalming; natural body care wherever local law allows
  • Biodegradable coffins, urns, and shrouds as standard
  • Preference for local suppliers and low-transport options
  • Paper-light processes and digital documentation
  • Support for tree planting and habitat restoration memorials
  • Clear, honest information about environmental impacts

If you are planning ahead, we can help you record your ecological wishes in a clear, simple plan so that your family feels supported when the time comes.
